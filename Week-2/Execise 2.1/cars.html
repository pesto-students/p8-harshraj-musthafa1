VW POLO

Volkswagen Polo Price: Volkswagen Polo price starts at ₹ 5.87 Lakh and goes upto ₹ 10.25 Lakh. The price of Petrol variant for Polo ranges between ₹ 5.87 Lakh - ₹ 10.25 Lakh and the price of Diesel variant for Polo ranges between ₹ 7.39 Lakh - ₹ 9.89 Lakh. Volkswagen Polo Versions: Polo is available in 23 variants. Out of these 23 variants, 15 are Manual, 6 are Automatic (TC) and 2 are Automatic. Volkswagen Polo Colours: Polo is offered in 5 colours: Sunset Red, Candy White, Carbon Steel , Flash Red and Reflex Silver. However, some of these colours are available in specific versions. Volkswagen Polo Competitors: Polo is competing against Hyundai i20, Tata Altroz, Hyundai Grand i10 Nios, Tata Tiago, Maruti Suzuki Swift, Renault Kwid, Maruti Suzuki Celerio, Tata Tiago NRG and Maruti Suzuki Baleno.
